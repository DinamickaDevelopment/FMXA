Market Leaders and Challengers

We love working with the big IT brands – especially when they come to us for something more exciting than they’ve run with before. It’s music to our ears.

Typically, our marketing teams will step in to invigorate a particular channel, such as SEO, social media or telemarketing, and often it is this part of a client’s campaign that sets the tone for the others

Growers and innovators

Working with a medium-sized tech business that wants to re-launch, diversify or enter a new market is always a rewarding campaign to work on. It’s where integrated marketing really comes into its own.

It’s all about fuelling the funnel. We start with awareness generation (e.g. events, PR, social, content, landing pages) and build on that with education and lead generation, using tactics that are both relevant and disruptive to propel our clients into their market’s sightline

Start-ups and smes

There is nothing more exciting than working with an ambitious tech start-up. In the past few years the world has seen small, bright sparks transform into world-dominating successes faster than ever before. It’s all down to great concepts and intelligent, creative marketing.

With smaller businesses, we typically take hold of the horns and manage the entire marketing function, working closely with the board. From concept and plan to execution and analysis, we use every marketing channel at our disposal to help smaller brands punch above their weight

There is no fixed way of working with us at FMXA. We appreciate each company we work with has a different need and budget but that each deserves the same level of service, regardless of size, ambition or scope
